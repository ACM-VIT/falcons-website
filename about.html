Who We Are ?

We manufacture radio controlled aircrafts which are designed and tested by the young students of our university with a mix of experienced faculty and is based on rich history of achievements. Our aircrafts specialize in surveillance and disaster management. The team also organizes routine workshops to create a sustaining environment of young aviation enthusiasts. The previous generations of aircrafts produced by the team received appreciation from the likes of Boeing, NASA and Lockheed Martin. We incorporate latest cutting edge research and manufacturing technologies for manufacturing the aircrafts. The entire team focuses on a single goal of popularizing aviation industry in the continent
